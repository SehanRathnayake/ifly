about us

iFLY enables young people to directly impact communities abroad through volunteer internships, as well as develop themselves personally and professionally by gaining practical skills and by experiencing a new culture..iFLY internships are typically 6-8 weeks and allow youth to make a positive impact in society in the areas of Cultural Understanding, Literacy, and Social Entrepreneurship.

Experience a new culture

While on exchange, one typically has the chance to thoroughly experience the culture of their host country.This excellent learning opportunity typically leaves an exchange participant with better understanding for people of all sorts, and can really make young people feel as though they have become “A GLOBAL CITIZEN”.

Meet new friends from all around the world

It is so much fun when exchange participants get together because you have so much in common. You will also meet tons of native friends in your exchange country that you will have a close relationship with for the rest of your life.

Differentiate yourself

International work and travel shows independence, responsibility, and a unique experience you can offer a company
